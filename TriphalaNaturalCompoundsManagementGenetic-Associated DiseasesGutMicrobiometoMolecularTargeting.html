Implications for Modern Medicine

By modulating fundamental cellular pathways like aquaporins, Triphala and similar natural compounds present promising avenues for addressing complex health challenges.

Combating Chemoresistance in Cancer

Many tumors overexpress aquaporins, helping them resist chemotherapy. Natural AQP modulators could potentially re-sensitize cancer cells to treatment, improving outcomes.

Targeting Metabolic and Neurological Health

Triphala's influence on aquaporins and inflammation suggests a role in managing conditions rooted in metabolic dysfunction and neuroinflammation, such as obesity and neurodevelopmental disorders.
